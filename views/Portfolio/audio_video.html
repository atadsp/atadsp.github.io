Audio Production and Post Production

The Adventures of Andrew Alexander Abraham and Fiona: Into the Snake pit

This is probably my favorite project that I worked on. The Adventures of Andrew Alexander Abraham and Fiona is based on a serialized radio drama. All the music in the clip are from the open source library of audio clips at incompetech.com . All the voice over work and audio editing was done by myself and the effects come from the student available library of audio clips provided by IU.

If the audio player fails to load, this clip is also available on youtube.


An Excerpt from Star Trek IV

This project was a particular challenge for myself. We had to find a section of a movie or TV show and completely recreate it. Being the huge Star Trek fan I am I looked through several of the scripts from my favorite movies and episodes from the series. I eventually settled on the trial scene from the beginning of Star Trek IV. I took the original scripted version and added a narration by Kirk to meet the time requirements. The music and sound effects are stock effects and tracks due to time constraints, but the ways in which they were inserted and manipulated are entirely of my own creation.
